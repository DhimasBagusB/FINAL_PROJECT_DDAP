BALI

Bali is a province in Indonesia whose capital city is Denpasar. The province of Bali is located in the western part of the Nusa Tenggara Islands. In the world, Bali is well-known as a tourism destination with the beautiful sights.

LOMBOK

Lombok Island is an island in Nusa Tenggara which is separated by the Lombok Strait from Bali to the west and the Alas Strait to the east from Sumbawa. Lombok is well-known as a tourism destination with the uniqueness of it's island.

RAJA AMPAT

The Raja Ampat Islands are a series of four adjacent islands located in the western part of the Bird's Head Papua Island. These islands are now a destination for divers who are interested in the beauty of the underwater scenery.
